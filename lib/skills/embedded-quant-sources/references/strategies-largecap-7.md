STRATEGY 1: MACD CROSSOVER MOMENTUM STRATEGY  
Entry (Buy) Rules

* MACD Line (12,26,9) crosses above Signal Line  
* MACD Histogram turns positive AND expands for 2 consecutive bars  
* Price closes above 20-period EMA to confirm trend alignment  
* Volume greater than or equal to 100% of 20-day average on crossover bar  
* RSI(14) between 40-70 (confirms momentum without overbought condition)

Exit (Stoploss) Rules

* Hard Stop: 1.5x ATR(14) below entry price OR below most recent swing low  
* Trailing Exit: Exit when MACD Line crosses below Signal Line AND Histogram turns negative  
* Profit Target: Previous resistance level OR when RSI(14) greater than 75 (overbought exhaustion)  
* Time Exit: Close position if no 2% move within 3 trading sessions

STRATEGY 2: RSI MEAN REVERSION STRATEGY  
Entry (Buy) Rules

* RSI(14) closes below 30 (oversold threshold)  
* Price touches or closes below lower Bollinger Band (20,2)  
* Next candle closes back inside Bollinger Bands (confirms reversal initiation)  
* Volume on reversal candle greater than or equal to 120% of 10-day average  
* Price remains above 200-day SMA (filters for long-term uptrend context)

Exit (Stoploss) Rules

* Hard Stop: 1.0x ATR(14) below the low of the reversal candle  
* Indicator Exit: RSI(14) crosses back above 50 AND price closes above middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA)  
* Profit Target: Middle Bollinger Band (initial) OR upper Bollinger Band (extended)  
* Invalidation Exit: Price closes below lower Bollinger Band for 2 consecutive sessions after entry

STRATEGY 3: BOLLINGER BANDS MEAN REVERSION STRATEGY  
Entry (Buy) Rules

* Price closes below lower Bollinger Band (20-period SMA, 2 std dev)  
* RSI(14) less than 35 confirms oversold condition  
* Next candle opens above prior close AND closes above lower band (reversal confirmation)  
* Volume on confirmation candle greater than or equal to 110% of 20-day average  
* ADX(14) less than 25 (confirms low-trend/range-bound environment ideal for mean reversion)

Exit (Stoploss) Rules

* Hard Stop: 1.2x ATR(14) below entry price OR below most recent swing low  
* Indicator Exit: Price closes above middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA) for profit-taking  
* Trailing Stop: Move stop to breakeven once price reaches middle band; trail at 0.8x ATR thereafter  
* Time Exit: Exit if price fails to reach middle band within 5 trading sessions

STRATEGY 4: VOLUME-CONFIRMED BREAKOUT STRATEGY  
Entry (Buy) Rules

* Price closes above defined resistance level (prior swing high, consolidation range high, or key moving average)  
* Volume on breakout candle greater than or equal to 150% of 20-day average volume  
* Breakout occurs during first 2 hours of trading session (higher conviction)  
* RSI(14) between 50-70 (confirms momentum without extreme overbought)  
* Price holds above breakout level on next candle's open (retest confirmation)

Exit (Stoploss) Rules

* Hard Stop: Place stop just below breakout level (0.5-1.0% buffer for noise)  
* Trailing Exit: Trail stop at 1.5x ATR(14) below highest close since entry  
* Volume Exit: Exit if volume on pullback candle greater than breakout volume (distribution signal)  
* Profit Target: Measured move \= height of prior consolidation pattern added to breakout point

STRATEGY 6: MACD \+ RSI DUAL CONFIRMATION STRATEGY  
Entry (Buy) Rules

* MACD Line (12,26,9) crosses above Signal Line (bullish momentum)  
* RSI(14) crosses above 50 from below (momentum confirmation)  
* Both signals occur within 3 trading bars of each other  
* Price closes above 20-period EMA  
* Volume on confirmation bar greater than or equal to 120% of 20-day average

Exit (Stoploss) Rules

* Hard Stop: 1.5x ATR(14) below entry OR below most recent swing low  
* Indicator Exit: EITHER MACD crosses below Signal Line OR RSI crosses below 45 (momentum loss)  
* Profit Target: When RSI(14) greater than 70 AND MACD Histogram begins contracting  
* Invalidation Exit: Price closes below 20 EMA for 2 consecutive sessions

STRATEGY 7: BOLLINGER BAND SQUEEZE BREAKOUT STRATEGY  
Entry (Buy) Rules

* Bollinger Band Width (20,2) at 6-month low (volatility contraction/squeeze)  
* Price closes above upper Bollinger Band with volume greater than or equal to 150% of 20-day average  
* ADX(14) begins rising from less than 20 to greater than 25 (trend emergence confirmation)  
* MACD Histogram turns positive on breakout candle  
* Breakout occurs after minimum 5-day consolidation pattern

Exit (Stoploss) Rules

* Hard Stop: Below middle Bollinger Band (20 SMA) OR 1.8x ATR(14) below entry (wider due to volatility expansion)  
* Trailing Exit: Trail at 2.0x ATR(14) below highest close once position is \+4%  
* Volatility Exit: Exit if Bollinger Band Width expands greater than 2x entry width AND price closes below middle band  
* Profit Target: 2x the height of the pre-squeeze consolidation range
